Try ELIZA

Talk to ELIZA

This is ELIZA running the genuine 1966 DOCTOR script: keyword ranking, decomposition and reassembly, equivalences, the memory rule, and the “certain counting mechanism” the recovered source revealed. Type a sentence and press enter.

Your conversation is private to your machine. Nothing you type is sent anywhere. Try opening with a feeling, or tell ELIZA about your mother. Type *help for the handful of commands. To see how each reply is built, step by step, visit How it works.

A faithful re-implementation in JavaScript, after the algorithm in Weizenbaum (1966) and the recovered MAD-SLIP source. It reproduces the canonical 1966 conversation.

THE COMPREHENSIVE VERSION

Anthony Hay’s ELIZA

For the definitive recreation, Anthony Hay’s ELIZA reproduces the original MAD-SLIP program’s behaviour exactly, down to the bugs, with full tracing, the original asterisk commands, the 1966 CACM replay, and the ability to load your own Weizenbaum-format scripts. It runs in the browser and is the most accurate ELIZA you can talk to.
